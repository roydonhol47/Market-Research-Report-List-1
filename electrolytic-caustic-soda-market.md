<p><strong>The Electrolytic Caustic Soda Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mercury Process</li><li>Diaphragm Process</li><li>Ion-Exchange Membrane Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electrolytic caustic soda market is categorized into three main types based on production processes. The Mercury Process uses mercury cells to produce caustic soda, but poses environmental concerns. The Diaphragm Process employs a porous diaphragm to separate chlorine and caustic soda, offering a safer alternative with lower mercury usage. Lastly, the Ion-Exchange Membrane Process utilizes selective ion-exchange membranes for higher efficiency and purity, making it increasingly popular for sustainable production while minimizing environmental impact.</p></p>
