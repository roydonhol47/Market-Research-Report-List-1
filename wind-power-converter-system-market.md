<p><strong>The Wind Power Converter System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Doubly-Fed</li><li>Full Power</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wind power converter system market is primarily categorized into two types: Doubly-Fed Induction Generators (DFIG) and Full Power Converter systems. DFIG technology enables variable-speed operation, allowing for efficient energy capture and reduced mechanical stress, making it cost-effective for medium to large wind farms. In contrast, Full Power Converters provide comprehensive control over the generator and grid interface, enhancing system reliability and performance, especially in areas with stringent grid requirements and for offshore wind applications.</p></p>
