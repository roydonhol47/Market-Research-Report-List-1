<p><p>The infrared optical lens market encompasses various lens types, including aspheric and spherical lenses. Aspheric lenses are designed with a non-spherical shape, minimizing optical aberrations and improving image quality, making them ideal for high-performance applications. In contrast, spherical lenses have a consistent curvature, making them simpler and often more cost-effective, but they may suffer from distortion at the edges. Both types serve critical roles in infrared imaging systems, sensors, and optical devices across diverse industries.</p></p>
