<p><p>The esophagus catheter market comprises various types, including balloon dilation catheters, irrigation catheters, and pressure monitoring catheters. Balloon dilation catheters are used to widen narrowed esophageal passages, improving swallowing. Irrigation catheters deliver fluids to cleanse or flush the esophagus, aiding in diagnostics or treatments. Pressure monitoring catheters assess the esophageal pressure dynamics, crucial for diagnosing motility disorders. Together, these catheters enhance patient care by addressing different esophageal conditions and facilitating effective interventions.</p></p>
