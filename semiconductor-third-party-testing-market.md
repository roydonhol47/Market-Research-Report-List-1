<p><p>The semiconductor third-party testing market encompasses various types of testing to ensure quality and reliability. Laboratory testing involves assessing semiconductor materials and devices for performance, reliability, and safety in controlled environments. Wafer testing focuses on evaluating individual semiconductor chips on a wafer before they are sliced and packaged, identifying defects early in the process. Final testing occurs after packaging, verifying the functionality and performance of completed semiconductor devices to ensure they meet industry standards before market release.</p></p>
