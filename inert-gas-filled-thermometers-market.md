<p><p>The inert gas filled thermometer market includes two main types: rigid stem and capillary thermometers. Rigid stem thermometers feature a solid structure, providing robustness and accuracy for high-temperature applications. They typically have a fixed length and are ideal for direct measurement in industrial settings. In contrast, capillary thermometers utilize a flexible capillary tube to transmit temperature changes, allowing for remote sensing. They are suitable for various applications where space constraints and flexibility are essential, offering precision in temperature monitoring.</p></p>
