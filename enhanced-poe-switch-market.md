<p><p>The Enhanced PoE Switch market comprises primarily two types: 802.3af PoE and 802.3at PoE+. The 802.3af PoE standard, also known as PoE, delivers up to 15.4 watts per device and is suitable for basic applications like IP cameras and VoIP phones. In contrast, the 802.3at PoE+ standard offers up to 30 watts, supporting more power-intensive devices such as PTZ cameras and wireless access points, enabling a broader range of applications and enhancing network efficiency.</p></p>
