<p><p>Freelance Management Software (FMS) is categorized into two main market types: cloud-based and on-premise solutions. Cloud-based FMS offers flexibility and accessibility, allowing users to manage freelancers from any location with internet access. This model typically involves subscription pricing and automatic updates. In contrast, on-premise FMS requires installation on local servers, providing greater control over data security and customization but necessitating more IT resources and maintenance. Businesses choose between these types based on their operational needs and IT infrastructure.</p></p>
