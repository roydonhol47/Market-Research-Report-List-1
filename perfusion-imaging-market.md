<p><strong>The Perfusion Imaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Computed Tomography (CT Scan)</li><li>Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI)</li><li>Nuclear Medicine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Perfusion imaging is a diagnostic technique used to assess blood flow in tissues and organs. The market is segmented into three main types: </p><p>1. **Computed Tomography (CT Scan)** - provides quick imaging and detailed anatomical information, ideal for evaluating cerebral perfusion. </p><p>2. **Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI)** - offers high-resolution images and functional data, useful for assessing perfusion in various organs, particularly in neurology and cardiology. </p><p>3. **Nuclear Medicine** - involves the use of radioactive tracers to visualize blood flow and is beneficial for evaluating conditions like heart disease.</p></p>
